1. Introduction

Meghtree Technologies ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website meghtree.co.in or use our services, in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023, the Information Technology Act 2000, and other applicable regulations.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name, work email, company name, and phone number submitted through our Cloud Efficiency Scorecard form
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide when contacting us

Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (anonymised where possible)
  • Referring URLs and pages visited
  • Date and time of access

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  1. Cloud Assessment Reports: To generate and deliver personalised Azure cost savings reports based on your scorecard responses.
  2. Communication: To respond to your inquiries, send assessment results, and provide updates about our services.
  3. Business Development: To follow up with you regarding our FinOps and cloud optimization services.
  4. Website Improvement: To analyse usage patterns and enhance our website experience.
  5. Compliance: To meet legal and regulatory obligations under applicable Indian and international law.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Formspree (our form submission processor) to deliver your scorecard responses to our team.
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate safeguards.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

HTTPS Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our website is encrypted using TLS.

Security Headers

We enforce Content-Security-Policy, X-Frame-Options, and other protective headers.

Secure Processing

All form inputs are sanitised and validated before processing.

Access Controls

Only authorised team members have access to submitted personal data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy. Scorecard submission data is retained for up to 24 months from the date of submission, unless you request earlier deletion. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@meghtree.co.in.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You may file a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality. We load Google Analytics (analytics cookies) only after you accept cookies via our site banner. We do not use marketing or advertising cookies. You can decline analytics cookies or clear your choice through your browser settings and local storage.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed by our service providers located outside India, including Formspree (United States). We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place for any cross-border data transfers, in compliance with the DPDP Act 2023.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
